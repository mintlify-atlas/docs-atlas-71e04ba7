## What is ErsatzTV?

ErsatzTV lets you transform your media library into a personalized, live TV experience - complete with EPG (Electronic Program Guide), channel scheduling, and seamless streaming to all your devices. Rediscover your content, your way.

Think of ErsatzTV as your personal TV network. Instead of scrolling through endless menus looking for something to watch, you can create custom channels that play your favorite content on a schedule - just like real television.

## Architecture

ErsatzTV is built with:

- **.NET 10** - Cross-platform server application
- **FFmpeg** - Media transcoding and streaming (custom FFmpeg builds with hardware acceleration support)
- **SQLite or MySQL/MariaDB** - Database backend
- **Blazor** - Modern web-based UI
- **HLS/MPEG-TS** - Industry-standard streaming protocols

### Components

**Main Server (`ErsatzTV`)**  
Handles the web UI, API, channel management, scheduling, and streaming. Runs on ports 8409 (default) for both UI and streaming.

**Scanner (`ErsatzTV.Scanner`)**  
Background process that scans media libraries, updates metadata, and maintains the search index.

**Transcoding Engine**  
Real-time FFmpeg-based transcoding with support for multiple hardware acceleration platforms.

## Use Cases

### Create Theme Channels
Build channels dedicated to specific genres, shows, or eras - like a 90s cartoon channel or a horror movie marathon.

### Simulate Broadcast TV
Schedule content with precise timing, filler content, pre-roll/mid-roll/post-roll sequences, and watermarks.

### Personal Music Channels
Create music video channels with your library, complete with schedule flexibility and visualization.

### Multi-Device Streaming
Stream to any IPTV client - smart TVs, Kodi, VLC, mobile apps, and more.
